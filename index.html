Selections from my designs for the #36DaysofType challenge 2023

The JK Serif Typeface was designed in Glyphs 3 and has 4 font weights with corresponding italic fonts. It's a traditional, yet elegant serif typeface designed to provide designers with a go-to choice with both display and text options for optimal versatility and legibility.

The Manteca Typeface was designed in Glyphs 3 and has 4 font weights with corresponding oblique fonts. It's a sans-serif typeface with high stroke-width contrast that evokes art deco design of the 1920s.

Flourish Oblique is a sans-serif font made in Glyphs mini meant to emhasize beauty and elegance. Potential use cases include companies like Disney looking for a font to use in marketing for a new movie with a central theme of beauty.

Papercut is a sans-serif smallcaps font designed in Glyphs 3 and Illustrator meant to look like it was made by cutting out the shapes with paper and scissors. It has a grunge feel to it that's perfect for alternative album covers.
